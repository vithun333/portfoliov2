I'm a UX designer and third-year Interactive Arts and Technology student at SFU, passionate about crafting intuitive digital experiences that blend research, Human-Computer Interaction principles, and visual design. I enjoy transforming complex problems into products that feel simple, engaging, and satisfying to use, with a strong focus on accessibility, usability, and meaningful user impact.

.inspiration

As a child, watching Tony Stark interact with J.A.R.V.I.S. sparked a fascination with the idea of technology that feels effortless and deeply human. In that moment, I knew I wanted to pursue a career dedicated to designing the kinds of intelligent, intuitive experiences that once felt like science fiction.
